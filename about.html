About

Wella_exe aka. Wella is an artist and designer from Germany.
She specializes in bright, colorful art. Her work varies from digital comic
styles to traditional paintings. She is also working on her own comic
series together with artist and creator @Tabesen. The comic is called
Big Wolf and little Hood. Her work is inspired by anime and cartoons,
street and graffiti art, by world cultures and folklore
and a touch of childlike imagination. Many works have been featured
in galleries and around her school.
Wella_exe is creating her own colorful and
mysterious world one piece at of art at a time.
